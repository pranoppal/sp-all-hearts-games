## Tips for Creating Good Crosswords

- Use words with common letters (E, A, R, T, O, N, S)
- Mix word lengths (short, medium, long)
- Avoid obscure words
- Write clear, unambiguous clues
- Add at least 8-10 words for a good puzzle
- Test the crossword by playing it yourself

## How to Add Words

1. Go to `/admin` in your browser
2. Enter the word (case doesn't matter)
3. Enter the clue
4. Click "Add Word"
5. Repeat for all words
6. Go to `/play` to test your crossword!
